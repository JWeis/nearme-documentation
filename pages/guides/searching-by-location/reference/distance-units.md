Distance units at your disposal when querying our server (often used in [radius](../radius), [point in shape](../point-in-shape)).

| Mile          | `mi` or `miles`                |
| Yard          | `yd` or `yards`                |
| Feet          | `ft` or `feet`                 |
| Kilometer     | `km` or `kilometers`           |
| Meter         | `m` or `meters`                |
| Nautical mile | `NM`, `nmi` or `nauticalmiles` |
